Rooms at Hotel PR Residency, Amritsar

Of all the Hotels in Amritsar you won't find anything that comes close to the Big Room Size and Design of rooms at Hotel PR Residency, Amritsar. Our hotel rooms provide everything you need for a comfortable and a good night's sleep. High ceilings, large windows and light-filled space are keynotes of the rooms and suites at Hotel PR Residency, Amritsar.

Our rooms have been designed to give you all the things you want for free like huge luggage space for families, big cupboards, unlimited access to free wi-fi, In-room dining and concierge service. Amritsar is a great place to visit and we help make your stay more comfortable with our services. Here's the  four different types of rooms we offer.
