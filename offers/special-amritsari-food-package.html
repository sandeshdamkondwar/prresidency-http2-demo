    Amritsar Food Package

    Day 1
    • Pick-up from Amritsar  Airport/ Railway Station in Air-Conditioned cars.
    • Priority Check-In at our well-appointed Deluxe Rooms
    • Elaborate Lunch at Hotel. (Amritsari Food Dishes to give you Amritsar's Feel )
    • After fulfilling Lunch, visit to Wagah Border Parade Ceremony. Be prepared to get goosebumps and get a sudden patriotic feeling.
    • If your tummy permits, head for famous Bhel-puri/Pani Puri (gol gappe) joint of Amritsar. Its surely different. Or you can go for special Kulfa Faluda (Punjabi Dessert)
    • Dinner for Vegtarians: Veg Thali at world Famous 'Kesar Da Dhaba' Amritsar .
    • Dinner for Non Vegetarians: Surjit Chicken/Makhan Fish (you tell us what is your mood to eat, and we would take you the right joint for that dish.)
    • Overnight stay at Hotel
    Day 2
    • Breakfast with world famous Amritsar Tandoori Kulcha Choley. (There are many amazing joints, but we will take you to THE BEST)
    • Visit to Jalianwala Bagh ,Golden Temple , Durgiana Temple and Shopping .
    • Lunch at Hotel PR Residency .
    • Evening Refreshment with famous Chat & Tikki joint.
    • Dinner for Vegetarians: Veg Thali at 'Bharawan Da Dhaba'
    • Dinner for Non Vegetarians: Mama Meat shop/ Beera Chicken.
    Day 3
    • For breakfast, make some space for the gastronomic Amritsar Puri-Aloo. Needless to say, it goes well with  glass of Punjabi Lassi.
    • One more visit to Golden Temple or go for shopping/leisure. Our chauffeur will take you to right places for authentic Amritsar shopping places.
    • Lunch At Hotel PR Residency . We will ensure to see you off on a Gastronomic note.)
    • Check-Out and Drop-off to Amritsar Airport/ Railway Station.

    Inclusions:

    • 3 Days/ 2Nights Accomodation in our well-appointed Deluxe Rooms.
    • All meals during the trip are included in the package cost. There will be around 9 full-meals meals i.e. (3 Lunches, 2 Breakfasts, 2 Dinners, 2 Evening Meals) + daily morning bed-tea.
    • Knowledgeable chauffeur cum guide accompanied visits to best eating joints in the foodie city of Amritsar. Leave it on us to suggest you the best items.
    • Package can be customized according to individuals eating preference (example, Vegetarian. Non Vegetarian, Tandoori Preference, Less Oily options etc.)
    • For any meal, if you are craving to have a particular dish, just tell us and we would take you to the best place to have that particular dish.
    • Welcome-Drink on Arrival
    • 2 Bottles of Mineral-Water .
    • All entrance fees to various spots and car parking fees.
    • All transfers in private Air-Conditioned cars.
    • Pick-up and drop-off from Railway-Station/Airport in Air-Conditioned cars.
    Note: All meals outside the hotel would be on fixed portion basis. They would be suffienct for anyone. Exceeding that or ordering outside of the set menu would be chargeable directly by the restaurant owner.

    Tips:

    • Leave 2 hours before the Wagha Border Parade start time for better seats and view.
    • Golden Temple experience at night is more mesmerizing than the day. Leave your shoes and belongings in the car when visiting the Golden temple, or you will have to keep them with the temple security.
    • Newly opened Trilium Mall (biggest by far in Punjab) is a great place to hangout. Its 5 mins away from the Hotel.
