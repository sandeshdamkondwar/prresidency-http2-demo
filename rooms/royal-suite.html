    Royal Suite

    Royal Suite at Hotel PR Residency, Amritsar, is the most magnificent and elegant room in the hotel, generously spread over an area of  450 sq.ft. Each suite offers you a relaxing living room and a vast bedroom equipped with a super comfy king-sized bed. The Suites are well designed with wooden flooring, separate living room and an exquisite marble bathroom with a bathtub/shower panel. Big picture frame windows capture the verdant views of Amritsar City. These suites create a soothing and warm atmosphere for you with modern guest amenities and services.
